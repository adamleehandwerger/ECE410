1 A kernel over subsets
\[
k(A,B)=\begin{cases}
    0 & \text{if } A\cup B =\emptyset \\
    \dfrac{|A \cap B|}{|A\cup B|} & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
\]

1. \[ k(A,B)=\begin{cases}
    0 & \text{if } A\cup B =\emptyset \\
    \dfrac{|A \cap B|}{|A\cup B|} & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
=\begin{cases}
    0 & \text{if } B\cup A =\emptyset \\
    \dfrac{|B \cap A|}{|B\cup A|} & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
=k(B,A)
\]
Hence K is symmetric.\\
